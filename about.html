Founder’s Vision

Led by Our Founder with Innovative Vision -Ambuj Singh

At Screentime Digital, we don’t just build brands — we create digital experiences that convert, connect, and stand out.

Founded by a creator-turned-strategist, we blend design, content, and marketing into one seamless solution for modern businesses and creators who want more than just likes — they want loyalty, leads, and lasting impact.

We’ve helped e-commerce brands launch clean, conversion-optimized websites. We’ve styled influencer shoots that spark engagement. And we’ve mapped out content calendars that turn reels into revenue.

Whether you're a startup founder or a personal brand, we meet you where you are — and build with you.

When I started Screentime Digital, it wasn’t just about design or marketing — it was about building a bridge between ideas and execution. I’ve seen countless talented creators and entrepreneurs struggle not because they lacked vision, but because they didn’t have the right system or support.

I’ve been there — starting from scratch, dreaming big, failing forward. What I realized is this:

The difference between noise and impact is intention.

Screentime Digital was born to bring clarity, structure, and soul into the online world. Whether you're launching your first product or scaling a 7-figure brand, my goal is to give you not just a creative agency — but a thinking partner who understands story, systems, and scale.

This isn’t just business.
It’s a mission to make meaningful brands win.

— Ambuj Singh
Founder, Screentime Digital
